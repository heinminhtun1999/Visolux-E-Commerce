# Visolux Store — User Manual (Customers)

**For:** Customers / shoppers (non-technical)

This guide explains how to browse products, checkout, choose delivery, pay, upload bank slips (if needed), and view receipts.

---

## 1) Browsing products

- Go to **Products** to view all items.
- Use search and category filters to find what you need.
- If an item is out of stock, you may not be able to add it to cart.

---

## 2) Cart

- Add items to your cart from the product page.
- In the cart you can change quantity or remove items.

---

## 3) Checkout (delivery + shipping fee)

### 3.1 Delivery address
At checkout, fill in:
- Name
- Phone
- Email
- Address line 1 / line 2
- City
- State (Malaysia)
- Postcode (5 digits)

### 3.2 How shipping is calculated
Shipping is calculated using **total cart weight**:

1) The system adds up the weight of all products in your cart.
2) Shipping is then quoted based on your **state/postcode** and the shop’s configured shipping rules.

If shipping is not available to your selected area, you will see a red message saying shipping is not available, and you can’t place the order until you change the delivery area.

---

## 4) Promo codes

- If you have a promo code, enter it at checkout.
- Some promos apply to item subtotal; some may apply to shipping (depending on the promo).

---

## 5) Payment methods

### 5.1 Online payment
- Choose **Online payment**.
- You will be redirected to the payment gateway.
- After payment, you will be redirected back to the store.

### 5.2 Offline transfer (bank slip)
- Choose **Offline transfer**.
- After placing the order, you will be asked to upload a bank slip.
- Admin will review your slip and approve/reject it.

Important:
- Make sure the slip image is clear.
- Uploading the slip does not automatically mean payment is verified.

---

## 6) Order history and receipts

### 6.1 Logged-in users
- Go to **Orders** to view your order history.

### 6.2 Guest checkout
- If you checked out as a guest, you can still view your order from the confirmation link.

### 6.3 Receipt
- The receipt shows items, totals, shipping fee, discount (if any), and total weight.

---

## 7) Refunds

If a refund is processed, your order status may change to:
- **Partially refunded**
- **Refunded**

---

## 8) Contacting the store

If you use the contact form:
- Your message is delivered to admins.
- Admins may mark the message as read or reply via the contact info you provided.
